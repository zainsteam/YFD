About Us

We are a team that builds websites, helps you show up on Google, and grows your brand through social media. Everything we do is shaped around your goals.

Your Vision, Our Expertise Together We Build

My name is Ram, and I’m the founder of Your Friendly Developers, an agency that helps businesses grow through website development, branding, and SEO. I wrote my first line of code at 15 and instantly fell in love with creating things through technology. After completing my degree in computer science, I launched this agency to build websites and digital solutions that are both effective and easy to use.

We believe every business has a unique story, and we’re here to help share it. From building clean, user-friendly websites to improving your online presence, we work closely with you to make sure your brand stands out and reaches the right audience.
